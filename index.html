Find Dry Chargers

For our first project in the UNH coding bootcamp, we created a web site which locates chargers for electric vehicles on long trips. Because these chargers are generally uncovered we provide the weather conditions at each charger which is in the direction he is going and at his battery range. The project is at this URL: https://github.com/Jay3172/Find_Dry_Chargers and is deployed at https://jay3172.github.io/Find_Dry_Chargers/ .
